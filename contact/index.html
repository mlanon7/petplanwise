Get in touch

We read every message. The fastest way to reach us is email — please include the page URL and a clear description if you're flagging a correction.

What to include

  • Corrections. Page URL + the figure or claim you're correcting + ideally a source.
  • Source pointers. If you've spotted newer industry data we should pick up, link us.
  • Partnership / affiliate. We're open to neutral comparison partnerships. We do not accept paid editorial placements that misrepresent products.
  • Press / data requests. Happy to help with citations as long as our methodology is linked.

Response time

Most messages get a reply within 5 business days. Time-sensitive vet questions should go to your veterinarian or your nearest emergency clinic — we're a planning resource, not a clinical one.

What we don't do here

  • Provide veterinary, financial, or legal advice.
  • Recommend specific clinics, insurers, or products by message — we publish those side-by-side.
  • Answer questions about a specific pet's medical case.
