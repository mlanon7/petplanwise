Pet insurance in Maryland

Maryland insurers quote $45–$80/month. Lyme disease is a material risk factor. DC metro premiums run higher than rural areas.

Frequently asked

How much does a dog cost per year in Maryland?

Maryland dog owners budget $1700/year for routine care, preventatives, and insurance. Emergency costs can exceed $5,000 per incident.

How does the DC metro affect Maryland costs?

Northern Maryland exurbs carry DC-level pricing (15–20% premium). Southern and rural Maryland are 25–35% below the state capital region.

Are vets cheaper outside the metro?

Yes. Rural practices in Maryland typically charge 15–25% less than major metros. Small towns and counties outside metro areas offer the lowest routine pricing.
