English Bulldog

English Bulldog cost calculator

English Bulldogs are among the most expensive popular breeds to own. Purchase: $1,500–$5,500. Annual: $2,800–$6,000+. Lifetime: $30,000–$60,000+ over a 9–11 year lifespan.

Quick answer

Buying a Bulldog from a breeder runs $1,500–$5,500. Annual ownership: $2,800–$6,000+. Lifetime: $30,000–$60,000+ over a 9–11 year average lifespan. Bulldogs carry one of the highest documented health-risk multipliers among popular breeds.

Bulldog-specific cost drivers

  • Brachycephalic airway syndrome (BOAS). Surgery to widen nostrils or trim the soft palate runs $1,500–$5,000.
  • Skin fold dermatitis. Recurring antibiotic and topical treatment, $200–$800/year.
  • Hip and elbow dysplasia. Surgical cases run $4,000–$8,000.
  • Heat sensitivity. Limits boarding and travel options. Many owners need AC accommodations.
  • C-section deliveries. Reflected in the breed's high purchase price.

Insurance fit

Strong fit. Bulldogs have one of the highest documented claim frequencies among popular breeds. Premiums run $80–$140/month for accident & illness coverage in most states. Compare carriers carefully and enroll while the dog is young to avoid pre-existing exclusions.
