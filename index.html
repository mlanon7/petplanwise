    Frequently asked

    How accurate are these calculators?

    They reflect current national survey averages and peer-reviewed price data, then add state, breed, age, and lifestyle adjustments. Each result shows a low / typical / high range so you see the spread rather than a falsely precise single number. For important decisions, get a written quote from your vet.

    Do you collect any personal data?

    No. The calculators run entirely in your browser. We don't ask for an email, we don't track you across the web, and your inputs never leave your device.

    How often are the numbers updated?

    We review every cost table at least quarterly against AVMA, NAPHIA, BLS CPI, and Banfield's State of Pet Health. Last full review: May 2026.

    Should I get pet insurance?

    Insurance is most useful as protection against large unexpected accident or illness bills. If your pet has even one major event over its lifetime, insurance often pays back many times over. Healthy pets may come out ahead with self-savings — use the Insurance vs Savings calculator to model both for your specific case.
