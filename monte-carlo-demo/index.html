How much could your pet really cost?

Everyone else gives you one average number. But it's not the average that hurts — it's the unlucky year. This runs 10,000 lifetimes of your pet and shows the whole range, including the tail nobody warns you about.

Illustrative demo. The incidence rates and cost ranges here are representative placeholders chosen to show the concept. The production version would use sourced data (OFA breed statistics, NAPHIA / insurer claims reports, AAHA, AVMA). This is a planning tool that models a range of possibilities — not a prediction, and not veterinary or financial advice.
