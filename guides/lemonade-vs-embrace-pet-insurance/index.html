Lemonade vs. Embrace Pet Insurance: Cost Comparison

Last updated: May 2026 · Methodology · Sources

Quick answer: Lemonade is the budget pick ($20–$50/mo, app-first, 90% reimbursement, accident & illness only, no wellness add-on). Embrace is pricier ($35–$80/mo) but includes optional wellness coverage and a unique healthy-pet diminishing deductible (drops $50 each year without claims).

When Option A wins

  • Budget-conscious: $20–$50/mo with 90% reimbursement is unbeatable for young, healthy pets
  • Tech-forward: prefer app-only claims (photo upload, instant submission)
  • No wellness interest: routine exams/vaccines aren't priorities
  • Simple coverage: illness + accident is all you need

When Option B wins

  • You want wellness bundled in (exams, vaccines, flea/tick, dental cleanings)
  • You value the diminishing deductible (rewards claim-free years: $500 → $450 → $400 over 3 years)
  • You prefer a full web + app portal and detailed claims support
  • Higher annual limits or unlimited coverage is a priority

The honest verdict

For young, healthy pets with no wellness interest, Lemonade is the smarter financial choice. For owners who want bundled wellness or reward loyal claim-free years, Embrace justifies the premium. Many owners switch between them; check portability and waiting periods before switching.

Common misconceptions

  • Lemonade doesn't cover chronic illness once diagnosed Lemonade covers ongoing treatment for accidents and illnesses diagnosed after enrollment. Pre-existing (diagnosed before enrollment) are excluded, like all insurers.
  • Embrace's diminishing deductible is automatic free money The deductible only drops if you have zero claims for 12 months. A single claim resets it. It's a loyalty reward, not a guarantee.

FAQ

Can I add wellness to Lemonade?

No. Lemonade offers accident & illness only. Embrace is the only option if you want wellness bundled. You can separately buy wellness via CareCredit or your vet.

How fast does Lemonade reimburse claims?

Typically within 3–7 days of claim approval. Embrace is similar (5–10 days). Both are faster than traditional insurers (4–8 weeks).

If I switch from Lemonade to Embrace, does my pre-existing condition status transfer?

No. Each insurer evaluates from scratch. A condition excluded by Lemonade will likely be excluded by Embrace if it's in your vet's records.
