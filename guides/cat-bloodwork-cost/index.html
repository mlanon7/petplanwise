How much does cat bloodwork cost?

Last updated: May 2026 · Methodology · Sources

Basic CBC: $80–$220. Comprehensive chemistry panel: $130–$380. Senior wellness packages: $180–$400.

Why senior cats need annual bloodwork

Cats hide illness well. Chronic kidney disease, hyperthyroidism, and diabetes are common in cats over 10 — and all three are far cheaper to manage when caught at the bloodwork stage instead of after symptoms. AAHA/AAFP feline life-stage guidelines recommend annual screening for adult cats and twice-yearly exams (with bloodwork) for seniors.

Insurance note

Diagnostic bloodwork for known illness is usually covered after deductible. Wellness/screening bloodwork usually requires a wellness add-on.

FAQ

What's SDMA and why is it on the bill?

SDMA detects early kidney function loss before traditional creatinine. It's now a routine line item in many feline senior wellness panels.
